About This Converter:

Purpose: This tool converts grades from your university's grading scale to the standardized 4.0 scale used by Ontario medical schools (OMSAS) and other professional programs.

Data Source: Conversion tables are based on the official OUAC (Ontario Universities' Application Centre) undergraduate grade conversion standards.

How to Use:

  1. Select your university from the dropdown menu
  2. Add your courses and enter your grades
  3. The 4.0 scale equivalent will be calculated automatically
  4. Click "Calculate Overall GPA" to get your cumulative 4.0 GPA

Important Notes:

  • Enter grades exactly as they appear on your transcript
  • For percentage-based scales, enter the numerical value (e.g., "85" for 85%)
  • For letter grades, enter the exact letter (e.g., "A", "B+", "C-")
  • This converter is for reference only - always verify with official sources
